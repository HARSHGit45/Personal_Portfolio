Project description

I worked on a Brain Tumor Classification system using shape features, achieving an accuracy of around 75%. This project aimed to develop a reliable system for distinguishing between benign and malignant brain tumors based on their morphological characteristics. By analyzing the shape features of the tumors, we trained the model to effectively classify the tumor types. Our approach provided a significant step forward in medical image analysis, offering a tool that can assist in the early diagnosis and treatment planning for brain tumor patients. The project demonstrates the practical application of machine learning in healthcare and its potential impact on improving patient outcomes.

Key Features:

  • Utilized shape features for accurate classification of brain tumors.
  • Achieved around 75% accuracy in tumor classification.
  • Classified tumors as benign or malignant.
